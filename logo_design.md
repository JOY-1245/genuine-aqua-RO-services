# Logo Presentation: Genuine Aqua Ro Tech Systems and Services

We have designed a modern, professional logo incorporating a blue theme suited for your water purification and filtration technology brand.

## Design Concept
- **Blue Theme**: Utilizes a professional gradient of deep navy blue (representing reliability, structure, and professional services) and vibrant cyan (representing purity, water, and technological advancement).
- **Emblem**: Features a sleek, abstract water droplet merged with filtration rings to represent RO (Reverse Osmosis) and technical services.
- **Typography**: Uses modern, minimalist, clean sans-serif typography for maximum legibility and a premium corporate feel.
